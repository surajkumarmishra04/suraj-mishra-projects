About B.Tech CSE (AI)

What is B.Tech CSE (AI)?

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence is a comprehensive program designed to build a strong foundation in core computing while specializing in modern AI technologies. It bridges traditional software engineering with advanced intelligent systems.

Course Duration

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as AI Engineers, Machine Learning Experts, Data Scientists, Software Developers, Systems Analysts, and Research Scientists in top-tier tech companies globally.
